Lets learn to use Zomato App

  1. Download and Install: First, download the Zomato app from your app store and install it on your Android device.

  2. Sign up or Log in:Open the app and either sign up for a new account or log in if you already have one.

  3. Set Location: Allow Zomato to access your location or manually enter your city to view nearby restaurants and food options.

  4. Explore Restaurants:: Browse through the list of restaurants, cafes, and food outlets available in your area. You can filter by cuisine, ratings, delivery, or dine-in options.

  5. Place an Order:Once you've chosen a restaurant, select the dishes you want to order and add them to your cart. Review your order and proceed to payment.

  6. Payment and Delivery:Choose your preferred payment method and complete the order. You can opt for delivery or pick up the order from the restaurant, depending on the available options.

That's it! Enjoy your delicious meal with the help of Zomato
